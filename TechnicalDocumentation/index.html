Crafts

There are a number of crafts that can be done with various fibers. Some of them are similar, while others are very different from each other.

  • knitting
  • crochet
  • cross stitch
  • embroidery
  • weaving
  • macrame

Tools

The various different crafts each use different tools. But there is one that is used in almost every craft: the sewing needle.

Other tools that are used include knitting needles, crochet hooks, embroidery hoops, and stitch markers.

Fibers

There are a variety of different fibers that can be used for the various crafts. Some of them are natural while some are sythetic.

Natural

Wool, bamboo, merino, cotton, cashmere, silk, hemp


Sythetic

Rayon, polyester, acrylic

Techniques

Once you learn the basics of each craft, you can add different techniques to add complexity and decoration to your projects.

Projects

There are a ton of projects that you can make in each craft. The sky and your imagination is really your only limit. Some examples include:

Knitting and Crochet

Apparel such as sweaters and vests, accessories such as hats, mitts, scarves, and boot cuffs. You can also make items such as blankets, pillows, and even stuffies.


Embroidery and Cross Stitch

These crafts can be done in hoops or frames and hung up as decorations. You can also embroider and cross stitch designs on pillowcases, towels, or items of clothing.
